SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of January 1, 2024, between TechCorp Solutions Inc. ("Service Provider") and ClientCo LLC ("Client").

ARTICLE 1: DEFINITIONS
1.1 "Services" means the software development and consulting services described in Exhibit A.
1.2 "Deliverables" means all work product, code, documentation, and materials created by Service Provider.
1.3 "Confidential Information" means any non-public information disclosed by either party.
1.4 "Term" means the period beginning on the Effective Date and continuing for twelve (12) months.

ARTICLE 2: SERVICES
2.1 Service Provider agrees to provide the Services described in Exhibit A in a professional and workmanlike manner.
2.2 Service Provider shall assign qualified personnel to perform the Services.
2.3 Client shall provide timely access to systems, information, and personnel as reasonably required.
2.4 Any changes to the scope of Services must be agreed in writing by both parties.

ARTICLE 3: COMPENSATION
3.1 Client shall pay Service Provider a monthly retainer of $25,000.
3.2 Additional services outside the scope shall be billed at $200 per hour.
3.3 Invoices are due within thirty (30) days of receipt.
3.4 Late payments shall accrue interest at 1.5% per month.

ARTICLE 4: INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
4.1 All Deliverables created specifically for Client shall be considered work-for-hire.
4.2 Upon full payment, Client shall own all rights, title, and interest in the Deliverables.
4.3 Service Provider retains rights to general methodologies, concepts, and know-how.
4.4 Service Provider may use generalized knowledge gained in future engagements.

ARTICLE 5: TERMINATION
5.1 Either party may terminate this Agreement with sixty (60) days written notice.
5.2 Client may terminate immediately for cause upon written notice.
5.3 Cause includes material breach not cured within fifteen (15) days of notice.
5.4 Upon termination, Service Provider shall deliver all work product to Client.
5.5 Client shall pay for all services rendered through the termination date.
5.6 Early termination by Client without cause requires payment of two months' retainer as a cancellation fee.

ARTICLE 6: CONFIDENTIALITY
6.1 Each party shall maintain the confidentiality of the other party's Confidential Information.
6.2 Confidentiality obligations survive termination for five (5) years.
6.3 Obligations do not apply to information that becomes publicly available through no fault of receiving party.

ARTICLE 7: WARRANTIES AND LIMITATIONS
7.1 Service Provider warrants Services will be performed in a professional manner.
7.2 Service Provider disclaims all other warranties, express or implied.
7.3 Total liability shall not exceed fees paid in the six months preceding the claim.
7.4 Neither party liable for indirect, consequential, or punitive damages.

ARTICLE 8: GENERAL PROVISIONS
8.1 This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties.
8.2 Governed by the laws of Delaware.
8.3 Disputes shall be resolved through binding arbitration in San Francisco.
8.4 Amendments must be in writing and signed by both parties.
